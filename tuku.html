Our Story

From Farm to Cup

Founded in 2015, TUKU began with a simple mission: to share exceptional coffee while supporting sustainable farming practices.

We travel directly to coffee-growing regions to build relationships with farmers who are as passionate about quality as we are. This direct trade approach ensures fair prices for growers while allowing us to select only the finest beans.
